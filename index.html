About

I'm Taher Ahmadi, a Machine Learning Engineer at Blue Boat Data, where I specialize in Large Language Models (LLMs) with a focus on automating data analysis for enterprises. I completed my master's degree in Computing Science at SFU. I was a member of Mars Lab, worked on Human Intent Prediction under the guidance of Prof. Mo Chen. also a member of ROSIE Lab supervised by Dr. Angelica Lim where I worked on Human-Robot Interaction.
I did a research internship at Hinge Health on 3D Human Pose Estimation. Prior to this I was a Data Engineer at Rahnema Co and a research assistant in the Cognitive Robotics Lab at Computer Engineering Department of Amirkabir University of Technology (Tehran Polytechnic).
